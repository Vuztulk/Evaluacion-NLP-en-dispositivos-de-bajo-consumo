Despair is a heavy burden that weighs on the heart. It's not just a fleeting feeling, but a deep-seated state of hopelessness. 
Despair is contagious, spreading like a shadow, touching everyone it comes into contact with. 
But despair is not just about sadness. 
It's also about defeat, the inability to see light at the end of the tunnel, to find hope in the midst of adversity.
